🔦 The Little Mole

In a big, dark place under the ground, a little mole liked to dig in the mud. He had a small map that showed secret tunnels.

2 / 6

🐷 A New Friend

One day, the mole met a pig.

"I can dig with you!" said the pig.

3 / 6

👨 The Friendly Man

Soon, a man came by. He had a net.

"I am not here to catch you. I want to help!" said the man.

4 / 6

🐜 The Clever Ant

As they walked, they saw an ant carrying a pen.

"I use this pen to draw paths," said the ant.

5 / 6

🫕 The Mysterious Pot

Then they found a pot in the mud.

"Let's dig!" said the mole.

The pig made a big hole. The mole dug fast. The man used his bat to tap the ground.

"Dig, dig, dig!" they all said.

Suddenly... CLINK!

6 / 6

🐕 What Is in the Pot?

The cat and the dog came to sit and watch.

"What is in the pot?" asked the dog.

"Is it gold? Is it food? Is it a map?" said the cat.

⭐ The End! ⭐
